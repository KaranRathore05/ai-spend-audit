# Go-to-Market Strategy

**Target user:**
- Founder, CTO, Head of Engineering, or engineering manager at a 2–50 person startup using multiple AI coding/writing/API tools.

**What they search/scroll before needing this:**
- “Cursor vs Copilot cost”
- “Claude API bill high”
- “OpenAI API cost optimization”
- “AI tools for startup team”
- “Why is our AI bill so high?”

**Where they hang out:**
- Hacker News
- Indie Hackers
- r/SaaS
- r/startups
- r/LocalLLaMA
- r/webdev
- X/Twitter founder and AI builder circles
- YC founder communities
- Product Hunt
- relevant Discord/Slack groups

**First 100 users with $0:**
- Post teardown thread on X analyzing popular AI stack configurations and where startups waste money.
- Share anonymized AI spend audits from early beta testers.
- Launch on Hacker News as “Show HN: AI Spend Doctor – Find wasted AI spend in 60 seconds”.
- Post in indie hacker communities with a focus on benchmarking tools.
- DM founders with a personalized “I audited your likely AI stack and found X savings” approach.
- Ask three interview users to share their public report URLs on their social feeds.
- Ensure the app provides high-quality, shareable result cards (Open Graph) to trigger viral loops.

**Unfair channel:**
- Credex already has access to AI credit supply and deep pricing knowledge across various providers, enabling unique insights.

**Week-1 traction expectations:**
- 300 landing visitors
- 80 audits completed
- 25 emails captured
- 5 high-savings consultation clicks
- 3 qualified Credex leads
