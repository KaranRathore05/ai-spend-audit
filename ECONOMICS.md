# Economics

**Assumptions:**
- Average qualified startup AI spend: $1,000/month
- Average possible savings through credits: 20–35%
- Credex gross margin estimate: 10–20%
- If a lead buys $12,000/year of credits and Credex margin is 15%, gross profit = $1,800/year
- If 100 audits produce 25 emails, 5 consultations, 1 purchase, then 1 customer from 100 audits
- CAC is near zero for organic channels but time cost exists

**Profitability:**
The tool is profitable if the time/maintenance cost per 100 audits is below the expected gross profit of the resulting customer conversions.

**To reach $1M ARR:**
- Need roughly $83k/month revenue
- If average customer buys $12k/year, need ~84 active customers
- If conversion rate from completed audit to paying customer is 1%, need around 8,400 completed audits over 18 months.

This unit economics model suggests the tool functions excellently as a top-of-funnel lead generator for Credex's core business model.
