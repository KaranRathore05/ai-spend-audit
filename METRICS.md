# Metrics

**North Star:**
Qualified savings discovered per week.

**Why:**
This matches the product’s core job: to find real AI overspend that can act as a trigger for qualified Credex leads. If the product finds substantial savings, users get value, and Credex gets high-intent leads.

**Input metrics:**
1. Audit completion rate
2. Email capture rate after results
3. High-savings consultation click rate

**Instrument first:**
- Landing page view
- Form started
- Audit completed
- Lead captured
- Public report shared
- Credex CTA clicked

**Pivot trigger:**
If after 300 completed audits fewer than 5% show meaningful savings or fewer than 2% click the consultation button, we should reposition the product toward benchmarking or education instead of direct lead-gen.
